[3/17/2026 5:57 AM] rumbledethumps
Many of you have expressed interest in a fully on-device experience for the RP6502. I propose that we collaborate on the project here.

@WojciechGw has already established a strong starting point, which you can find here: https://github.com/WojciechGw/cc65-rp6502os. Projects like GeckOS and LUnix also offer excellent ideas that map well to the RP6502 architecture.

I plan to stay on the sidelines to provide coaching and ensure the platform remains supportable. To avoid future confusion with the existing RP6502-OS name, part of the RP6502-RIA, RP6502-RIA-W, RP6502-VGA family, I suggest we begin by choosing a unique name for this project.

So let's get things started with bikeshedding the name.
